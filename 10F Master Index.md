## Key Themes Across Forecasts

- **Fragmentation:** Nearly all forecasts describe movement from unified to fragmented systems
- **Strategic Opacity:** Information control emerges as critical competitive advantage (01, 06)
- **Technology Sovereignty:** Nations pursuing independent tech capabilities (04, 10)
- **Resource Power:** Energy and economic systems fragmenting (08, 09)
- **Human Capital:** Migration as strategic asset (07, 03)
